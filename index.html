Our Methodology

Vibe Coding: AI That Matches How You Think

Vibe coding is rapid AI prototyping that builds systems matching how your business actually operates—not generic templates or cookie-cutter solutions.

We build working prototypes in 60-90 minute sessions, test with real data immediately, and iterate based on actual usage. You see progress weekly, not just at the end.

Core principles:

  • • Human-centered design: AI captures your voice, thinking patterns, and decision-making process
  • • Rapid prototyping: Working features in days, not months
  • • Agile delivery: 1-week sprint cycles with continuous feedback
  • • Sustainable implementation: Realistic timelines that respect team capacity and prevent burnout

Agile AI Framework: Short Cycles, Early Wins

Traditional AI projects fail because they take too long, cost too much, and deliver everything at once. Our Agile approach gives you working automation in Week 2.
